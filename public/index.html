FAQ

How does QGenie generate questions?

QGenie uses a trained GPT model to generate questions based on specific topics in each subject's specification.

Is QGenie only for UK students?

Whilst the exam boards are based in the UK, the questions generated can still apply to international students

How many questions can I generate with QGenie?

QGenie generates questions in batches of 5. The amount of questions depends on the tier of your account.
